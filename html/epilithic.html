Epilithic

Spring/Summer 2025

More coming soon!!

Epilithic is a brand and website that serves creatives. Specifically, it is a community website for graphic designers (and eventually all types of designers and artists) to post inspiration, tools, techniques, and other things that help with the design process (i.e. books, movies, TV, music). The website will feature a community blog for anyone to post on, but there should also be a large sense of community where people can connect. This online space is supposed to feel like an older version of social media and the internet, reminiscent of sites like Tumblr, MySpace, or DeviantArt. The focus is on community and artistic growth without the pressure of gaining popularity or monetization.
