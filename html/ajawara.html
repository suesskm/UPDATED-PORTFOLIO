Ajawara Client Website

Winter 25

Web Design, UX/UI Design, Branding

Ogechi Ajawara needed a professional and visually compelling digital portfolio to establish her online presence, showcase her expertise in marketing, and attract potential clients. The challenge was to create a website that not only reflects her skills and personality but also provides an engaging and seamless user experience across different devices.

I designed and developed a responsive website that balances professionalism with personality through a muted color palette accented with bright tones. The UX/UI design ensures intuitive navigation, while the branding elements reinforce Ogechi’s unique identity. Built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the site is optimized for both desktop and mobile, offering a polished and engaging experience that instills confidence in her skills and services.

The brand guide for Ogechi Ajawara was developed to establish a cohesive and professional identity that reflects her expertise and personality. This guide includes typography, color palettes, branding elements, wireframes, and a website flowchart, all of which contribute to a seamless and engaging digital presence.

Wireframes for both desktop and mobile views were created to structure the website efficiently. The focus was on:

Intuitive Navigation: Ensuring users can quickly find key information such as work samples, resume, and contact details.

User Experience: A streamlined layout that enhances usability across devices.

Visual Hierarchy: Organizing content effectively to guide visitors through the portfolio with ease.

The wireframes define the foundation of the website’s layout, prioritizing clarity and accessibility while maintaining a visually appealing structure.

A sitemap and user flowchart were created to illustrate the user journey, mapping out how visitors navigate through the homepage, work portfolio, resume, and contact section. This strategic planning ensures a seamless interaction between the website’s design and functionality, allowing for an engaging user experience.
