The Perfect Cup Website

Summer 2024

Web Design, UX/UI Design

I was tasked with creating a website for The Perfect Cup, a renowned coffee shop in Chicago. The process began with curating images and crafting the copy for the site. Next, I developed a user flow map to outline the website's structure and used this information to create detailed wireframes.

Using a combination of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I then built the website, ensuring it was visually appealing and user-friendly. The site features a clean, minimalist design, with a color palette inspired by the shop's logo. I also incorporated a responsive design to ensure the site is accessible on all devices.
