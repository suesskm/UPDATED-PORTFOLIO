Nature & Tech

Spring 2025

I wanted to explore the concept of juxtaposition, specifically the contrast between nature and technology. Using Adobe After Effects, I experimented with a new technique that brings movement to life through pixels and keyboard inputs. I incorporated original footage of flowers and trees to highlight the organic element, blending it with digital motion to emphasize the tension and harmony between the two worlds.
