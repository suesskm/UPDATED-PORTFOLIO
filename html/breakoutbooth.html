BreakoutBooth Podcast – Branding Process

When creating the brand identity for the BreakoutBooth Podcast, I focused on building a dynamic and modern aesthetic that aligns with the podcast’s mission—breaking barriers, fostering conversation, and embracing bold ideas.

Concept & Vision
The core concept behind BreakoutBooth was to craft a brand that feels energetic, engaging, and innovative. The name itself conveys a sense of disruption and movement, which informed the visual language and design choices. I wanted the identity to feel fresh, adaptable, and versatile across different mediums, from digital interfaces to merchandise.

Logo & Typography
The logo embodies the idea of breaking through limitations, using a simple yet powerful symbol that reflects motion and progress. I opted for a clean, contemporary typeface—Neue Haas Grotesk—to ensure readability while maintaining a bold, modern look. The typeface’s versatility makes it effective across various applications, from app icons to promotional materials.

Color Palette
The color scheme is a carefully curated mix of vibrant neons and muted tones, creating a balance between playfulness and professionalism. Shades of lime green, electric blue, and soft purples provide a dynamic and eye-catching contrast that makes the brand stand out. These colors were chosen to reflect energy, creativity, and a forward-thinking attitude.

Brand Applications
The branding extends beyond just a logo and color scheme. I ensured that the design translates well across different formats:

Mobile & Digital Presence: The app icons and interface elements are designed to be visually cohesive and instantly recognizable.

Social Media & Marketing Assets: Graphics incorporate abstract patterns and vibrant backgrounds to maintain an engaging aesthetic.

Merchandise & Physical Branding: The brand identity is adaptable for real-world applications, such as tote bags and hats, reinforcing its versatility.

The BreakoutBooth Podcast brand identity is built to be bold, recognizable, and adaptable. Every element—from typography to colors and layouts—was carefully selected to enhance the podcast’s presence and align with its purpose of pushing boundaries and fostering meaningful discussions.
