Design Forum Website

Fall 2024

Design Forum is an interactive online community where graphic designers can connect, collaborate, and immerse themselves in the world of design. The platform fosters creativity by making graphic design more approachable and social, creating a welcoming space for both seasoned designers and newcomers to the field. Users are encouraged to embark on their own creative journeys by exploring and interacting with various clickable objects and 3D models.

The website is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, seamlessly blending 2D elements with an immersive 3D environment. This combination enhances functionality and user interaction, transforming the site into an engaging, dynamic space that encourages exploration and creativity.
