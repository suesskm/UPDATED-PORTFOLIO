Other Dan's Ex-Girlfriend

Spring 23

Poster Design, Branding, Marketing

Other Danâ€™s Ex-Girlfriend is an independent short film set in Chicago. The story follows the main character on a whimsical and otherworldly journey throughout the city after she locks herself out of her apartment.

In collaboration with the New Generation Filmmakers Forum and the Davis Theater, I took on the marketing efforts for the film, which included designing commemorative postcards, developing cohesive branding, and creating a program for distribution in the theater.

For the poster design, I aimed to capture this sense of whimsy and otherworldliness. I hand-drew the entire poster, scanned the sketches, and digitized them using Adobe Illustrator. The background is a composite of three different images created with pastel and colored pencils.
